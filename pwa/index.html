How it works

1
Your browser creates a passkey — a cryptographic key pair where the private key is locked inside your device's secure enclave and automatically syncs to iCloud Keychain or Google Password Manager.
2
The public key is hashed with SHA-256. Three indices are derived from the hash and mapped to symbols from a curated pool of 5,390 Unicode characters.
3
The result is your identity — deterministic, verifiable, and owned only by you. No server involved. No email. No password.
